Mission goal
============

As a new member of the Royal Guard, you need to learn about security basics. The 
Captain of the Guard has noticed that many guards leave traces of their commands 
in their magical scrolls (bash history).

Your mission is to:
1. Check what commands are stored in your history
2. Learn how to clear sensitive commands
3. Clear a specific password command from your history

Useful commands
==============

history
    Shows the command history

history -d NUMBER
    Deletes a specific command from history (NUMBER is the command's position)

history | grep PATTERN
    Searches for specific commands in history
