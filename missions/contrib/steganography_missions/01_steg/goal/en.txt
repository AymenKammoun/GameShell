Mission goal
============

A spy of the king discovered the identity of a traitor who attempted to poison His Majesty. 
To protect this sensitive information, the spy hid the traitor's name inside a painting in 
the throne room.
The spy indicated to the king that the passphrase is "Tra1t0r".

Your mission is to find the traitor's name hidden in the painting.

Useful commands
==============

eog IMAGE
    Opens a preview of the IMAGE in Linux

COMMANDE &
  Run the command in the background.

steghide extract -sf FILE -p PASSPHRASE
    Extracts hidden data from FILE using the PASSPHRASE used to initally embed the data 
    inside the FILE.
