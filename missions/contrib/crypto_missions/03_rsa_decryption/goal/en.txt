Mission goal
============

Your friend King James want to send you an encrypted letter.
You have agreed to use RSA algorithm, for that you have to generate a paire of public/private keys.
Then send your public key to King James so that he can reply back with the encrypted lettre.
Finaly, decrypt the lettre using your private key.

Useful commands
==============

openssl genrsa -out private.pem 4096
openssl rsa -in private.pem -pubout -out public.pem

openssl pkeyutl -encrypt -inkey public.pem -pubin -in top_secret.txt -out top_secret.enc
openssl pkeyutl -decrypt -inkey private.pem -in top_secret.enc -out top_secret2.txt
